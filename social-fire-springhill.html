Springhill Suites — Panama City Beach

Beachfront fire, right out the door

Every Monday night, the LMU team sets up a private beach bonfire right outside Springhill Suites. Your fire is already burning when you step outside. No hauling, no hassle — just show up and enjoy.

Everything is handled — permits, setup, the fire itself, chairs, tiki torches, s'mores, and full cleanup. All you bring is yourself.
