What is a Social Fire?

All the magic, none of the logistics

No group? No problem. Join a beach bonfire by the seat — no need to book a private fire or round up a crowd.
From $45 per person. Get the full Light Me Up experience for a fraction of the private bonfire price. Same fire, same s'mores, same magic.
Need a final day plan? Book same-day and show up ready to enjoy.
