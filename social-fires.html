Social Fires at your hotel

A private beach bonfire experience — set up, staffed, and broken down by the LMU team — right outside your hotel. No hauling, no hassle.

"A fire on the beach, right outside your hotel. Just walk out and enjoy the night."

Social Fires are private beach bonfire experiences hosted at select hotel properties along 30A and Panama City Beach. We partner directly with these hotels so you can enjoy everything LMU does best — the fire, the setup, the atmosphere — without ever leaving your resort. Click your hotel below to learn more and book.

What is a Social Fire?

All the magic, none of the logistics

Set up on-site. We coordinate directly with your hotel and set everything up on the beach access right at the property.
Full LMU experience. Same bonfire, same s'mores, same permits and cleanup — everything included.
Private and exclusive. Your fire, your group, your night. Hotel guests only.
Add-ons available. Photography, glow sticks, extra time, s'mores packages — all the extras you love.
