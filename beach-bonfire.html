Catering

Important Catering Note

Walton County now considers any food delivered to beach accesses as "catering," which requires special event permitting. We suggest bringing your own food to avoid any last-minute changes to your reservation.

Please note: Nothing can be delivered directly to the access. Please have food delivered to your house and bring it with you, or pick it up on the way.

If you're booking a larger event or are interested in catering, we're happy to provide options. Base price for catering / food delivery:

$300 + cost of food & any additional caterer fees
