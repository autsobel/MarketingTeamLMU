Our Story

Family owned & operated since 2015

Hey there!

We are Light Me Up Beach Bonfires! Our family owned and operated business was established in 2015 as one of the very first beach bonfire companies on the Gulf Coast. Over the years, we have transformed this sweet industry into what it is today.

We offer beach bonfires, picnics, dining, special events, back-yard and in-home parties, beach chair rentals, and more — from Panama City Beach, Walton County, and all the way along 30A. Scroll down and click on your location to view our packages, pricing, and to inquire or book.

As always, we look forward to serving you, your friends, and your families. See you on the beach!

FAQ & Policies

Good to know

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellations made 72 or more hours in advance receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, a 50% credit is issued toward a future booking. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable, but we are always happy to work with you on rescheduling when possible.

What if there is bad weather? +

Safety always comes first. If weather conditions prevent a safe bonfire — high winds, lightning, or a red-flag beach day — we will work with you to find a new date at no extra charge. We monitor conditions closely and will reach out as early as possible.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 3 to 5 days in advance, especially during peak season from June through August. Last-minute requests are sometimes possible — just reach out and we will do our best to make it happen.

Do you handle all the permits? +

Every package includes the beach bonfire permit for your location. We take care of all required county and local permits so you do not have to think about it. Just show up and enjoy the fire.

Do I need to bring anything? +

Not a thing. Everything you need is included in your package. We arrive before you, set everything up, and clean up afterward. All you need to bring is yourself and the people you love.

What add-ons are available? +

You can add roses for the One Love package, extra chairs, beach umbrellas, chalkboard signs, sparklers, cornhole, photography sessions, and floral arrangements. You can select your add-ons when you fill out the booking form above.
